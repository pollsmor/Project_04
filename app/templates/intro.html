Our webpage compares the epidemics in recent years. What's special about our webpage is that it compares the epidemics day by day since the beginning of each outbreak, which shows us how severe each disease is. On the y-axis, it shows total deaths and the x-axis shows number of days. Our graph is easy to decipher For example, one can easily see that COVID-19 seems to be the most severe from the curve, while the MERS disease seems to be the least severe based on the curve.
